This post confirms the blog is enabled again.

If you based your site on **al-folio** ([repo](https://github.com/alshedivat/al-folio)) and previously disabled the blog, the key is to have at least one post in the `_posts/` folder and keep the blog page (`/_pages/blog.md`) in the navbar.
